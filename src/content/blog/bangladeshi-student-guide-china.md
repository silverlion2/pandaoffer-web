## Why Bangladeshi Students Choose China

China has become the **top destination for Bangladeshi students seeking affordable, quality education** outside of South Asia. Over **8,000 Bangladeshi students** currently study in China, drawn by CSC scholarships, low tuition, WHO-recognized MBBS programs, and the growing economic ties between the two countries.

## CSC Scholarship for Bangladeshis

### Application Route

Bangladeshi students can apply through:

1. **Bangladesh Embassy in Beijing** (Type A) — apply via the embassy's annual announcement
2. **Directly to the university** (Type B) — many universities have separate CSC quotas

### Timeline

- **October–November**: Research universities and contact professors
- **December–January**: CSC portal opens, prepare documents
- **January–February**: Embassy deadline (Type A)
- **March–April**: University deadline (Type B)
- **May–July**: Results announced

### Key Tips

- **Professor pre-acceptance letters** dramatically increase success — start emailing professors 3–6 months early
- **Study plan quality** matters more than GPA in Bangladesh — be specific about your research goals
- **IELTS scores** are helpful but not always mandatory — check each university's requirements
- **SSC + HSC certificates** need attestation from the education board and Ministry of Foreign Affairs

## Visa Process from Bangladesh

### Steps

1. Receive admission letter + JW202 form
2. Complete physical exam at a DGHS-approved hospital
3. Apply at the **Chinese Embassy in Dhaka**
4. Bring: passport, admission letter, JW202, physical exam report, study plan, photos
5. Processing: 4–7 business days
6. Cost: Approximately BDT 5,000–8,000

### Money Transfer

- **bKash → Wise → Chinese bank** — most affordable route
- **Western Union** — reliable but higher fees
- Open a **Bank of China or ICBC account** within your first week for WeChat Pay and Alipay

## Student Community & Life

### Finding Fellow Bangladeshis

- **Bangladesh Student Associations** exist at most universities with significant international populations
- **University WhatsApp/WeChat groups** — ask the admissions office upon arrival
- **Eid celebrations**: Universities with Muslim student communities typically organize events
- **Halal food**: 清真 restaurants and Lanzhou noodle shops are available everywhere

### Cultural Adjustment

- **Rice is the staple** in both cultures — canteen food will feel somewhat familiar
- **Tea culture**: Both Bangladesh and China are tea-loving nations — bond over tea
- **Bargaining**: Common in Chinese markets, just like in Bangladesh
- **Weather**: If coming from Dhaka, northern Chinese winters will be a shock — invest in warm clothing immediately

### Best Cities for Bangladeshis

1. **Wuhan** — Most affordable, large international community
2. **Beijing** — Largest Bangladeshi community, embassy access
3. **Nanjing** — Good universities, moderate cost
4. **Kunming** — Warm weather year-round, very affordable
